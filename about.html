About Hooke Farm Wilding

Hooke Farm is a place where the “tidy” line gets softened on purpose—so insects, birds, mammals, fungi, and wildflowers can get on with the real work of making a landscape sing.

Wilding here isn’t about abandonment—it’s about attention. Making space for shelter, food, and complexity, then letting the land do what it does best: knit together life in layers.

About this app

The Wilding Portal is an offline webapp that turns info boards into an interactive guide: a clickable map, searchable cards, readable text, seasonal notes, and photo galleries.

Admin mode lets you curate locally: edit titles and stories, add and remove photos, move pins, hide features, or delete them permanently. Everything is saved in your browser storage.
